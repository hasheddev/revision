Joins are used to combine data about an object to get a bigger picture about this object like customers, addresses, orders and reviews.
They are used for Data enrichment to get extra data about an entity

And also for checking existence of a record.

Joins result in three scenarios: Matching data from both tables, All Data From one table and some from the other and Only unmatching data from one table Only(Data present in one but not the other)

Types:
  Basic: Inner join, Left Join, Right Join, Full Outer Join.
  Advanced:  Left Anti Join, Right Anti Join, Full Anti Join and Cross Join
